Feeduce

is our first app



Feed .. Reduce .. FeedUce


FeedUce is a platform for the donors (restaurants, hotels, food industries…) and the charities to connect with each other. The donors can log in and post about the extra meals/ food they’re trying to get rid of. In the meanwhile, charities, once logged in, they can check the different posts and search for a nearby restaurant/hotel to pick up the food.
